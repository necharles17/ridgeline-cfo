Why a fractional CFO?

A fractional CFO gives you executive-level financial leadership on a flexible, part-time basis. You get the decision support, financial modeling, and strategic thinking of a full-time CFO without the full-time overhead.

A fractional CFO looks forward to help you plan, forecast, and make better decisions with your money so you can focus on growth and profitability.

Frequently asked questions

Do you replace my bookkeeper or tax accountant?

No, and that's exactly the point. Your accounting team handles the historical records of what has already happened while we sit on top of that data to turn it into forward-looking strategy, forecasts, and decision frameworks. We act as the “translator” between your books and your vision, working closely with your existing bookkeeping, accounting, and tax teams.

What size companies do you work with?

We partner with growth-stage businesses, typically with revenues between $2M and $50M. This is often the range where financial complexity has outpaced your current infrastructure and your decisions are based on “gut feel”, not real data.

Outside of this range? We also work on one-time projects that fit your needs if a monthly retainer plan isn't needed. Let's chat.

How much does this cost?

Our model is designed to be a fraction of the cost of a full-time CFO, which can exceed $300k per year once salary, benefits, and incentives are factored in.

While every scope is unique, ongoing monthly retainers typically range from $2,000 to $10,000 per month.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most clients begin with our Diagnostic Phase, a deep-dive into your financial infrastructure to find the gaps. From there, we transition into a customized ongoing retainer plan designed to provide the specific level of strategic leadership and accountability your business requires.

How is this different from hiring a consultant?

Traditional consultants drop in, deliver a static report, and leave you to figure it out alone. We embed as an ongoing part of your team with regular strategy calls and 24/7 Slack access. We pride ourselves on being responsive “builders” who get you the answers you need so you can get back to running your business and your life.
