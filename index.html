Why a fractional CFO?

A fractional CFO gives you executive-level financial leadership on a flexible, part-time basis. You get the decision support, financial modeling, and strategic thinking of a full-time CFO without the $300k+ full-time overhead.

A fractional CFO looks forward to help you plan, forecast, and make better decisions with your money so you can focus on growth and profitability.

We don't just hand you reports. We make sure you understand every number and why it matters, so you can make decisions with confidence.

Frequently asked questions

Do you replace my bookkeeper or accountant?

No, and that's exactly the point. Your bookkeeper handles the day-to-day transaction recording and your CPA manages taxes. We sit on top of that foundation as your strategic CFO layer, turning historical data into forward-looking forecasts, cash flow models, and decision frameworks. We work alongside your existing team, not instead of them.

What size companies do you work with?

We partner with businesses in the $1M–$35M revenue range. This is the sweet spot where financial complexity has outpaced your current systems, but you're not ready for a $300k+ full-time CFO.

Outside this range? We also offer project-based advisory work if a monthly retainer doesn't fit your needs. Let's chat.

How much does it cost?

A full-time CFO can exceed $300k per year once salary, benefits, and incentives are factored in. Our model gives you CFO-level expertise for a fraction of that cost.

Our engagements start at $1,500/month and go up to $10,000/month depending on your scope, goals, and required support. We also offer project-based pricing for one-time engagements.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most clients choose one of our three monthly retainer tiers based on their current needs: Foundation for accountability and clarity, Growth for active forecasting and strategy, or Premier for comprehensive financial partnership with unlimited access. We customize the scope within your tier to fit your specific business challenges and growth stage.

How is this different from hiring a consultant?

Traditional consultants drop in, deliver a report, and leave you to figure it out alone. We embed as an ongoing part of your team with regular calls and 24/7 Slack access. We're responsive builders who get you the answers you need so you can get back to running your business and your life.
