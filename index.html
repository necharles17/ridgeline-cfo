Why a fractional CFO?

A fractional CFO gives you executive-level financial leadership on a flexible, part-time basis. You get the decision support, financial modeling, and strategic thinking of a full-time CFO without the full-time overhead.

A fractional CFO looks forward to help you plan, forecast, and make better decisions with your money so you can focus on growth and profitability.

Frequently asked questions

How much does this cost?

We get it — you've been burned by vague pricing before. Engagement cost depends on scope and complexity, and every business is different. We'll scope it together on a free intro call so there are no surprises. Most clients find the cost is a fraction of the full-time CFO they can't justify yet.

Do you replace my accountant?

No — and that's exactly the point. Your accountant handles what already happened. We handle what happens next. We sit on top of their work and turn it into strategy, forecasts, and decision frameworks. If your financial statements currently feel like a foreign language, we're the translator.

What size companies do you work with?

Businesses where growth has outpaced the financial infrastructure. You're generating real revenue, making real decisions, and feeling the weight of both — but a $250K+ full-time CFO doesn't make sense yet. If uncertainty feels like a constant companion when it comes to your finances, we're built for you.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most clients start with the Diagnostic phase — a focused deep-dive into your financial infrastructure. From there, we move into an ongoing tier (Performance & Oversight or Growth & Forecasting) based on what your business actually needs.

How is this different from hiring a consultant?

Consultants drop in, deliver a report, and leave — and you're back to figuring it out alone. We embed as an ongoing part of your team with 24/7 Slack access, attending your meetings, building your models, and helping you make decisions in real time. When a big question lands on your desk at 9 PM, you have someone to call.
